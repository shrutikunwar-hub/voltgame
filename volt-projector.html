CASE CLOSED
VOLT Nova. 2018–2023. Strategically deceased. Survived by four very avoidable mistakes.
Round 1 — SCP / Porter's 5 Forces.
I entered a war zone dressed for a photoshoot. More rivals = lower prices. Industry structure decides your fate before you show up. I showed up unprepared and called it confidence.

Round 2 — VRIO.
I invented something brilliant, held a press event about it, and then gave it away for free because I forgot to patent it. Resources that can be copied aren't advantages. They're very expensive lessons.

Round 3 — Value Creation (WTP).
People loved me. They also left me. Completely unbothered. No switching cost, no network effect, no loyalty mechanism. Being the favourite is not the same as being irreplaceable.

Round 4 — Trade-offs.
I was winning the wrong game. Enthusiastically. With a great dashboard and a cake nobody can explain.

Strategy isn't about being the best. It's about being hard to beat. I was neither. You're welcome for the lesson.
